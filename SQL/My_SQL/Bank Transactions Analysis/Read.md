**Bank Transaction Analysis**
This project was done with a motive of understanding how banks usually operate in diverse perspectives of customer transaction activities,fraud detection,monitor account activities and generation of business insights.The dataset was a small sample selected from a certain bank record.

**KPIS**
•	Total Transactions

•	Total Deposits

•	Total Withdrawals

•	Average Transactions

•	Net Cash flows

•	Currents Account Balances

•	Bank Branch Balances

•	Peak hours and transaction types
