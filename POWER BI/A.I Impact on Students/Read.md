☎** HR ATTRITION DASHBOARD**

This Project focuses in analyzing the impact of Artificial Intelligence(A.I) impact on learning students. During the modern times ,Artificial Intelligence (A.I) has been prevalent in many aspects of life including the education sector.The main agenda of this project was to unveil how AI was  affecting the  overall performance of  students and formulating reasonable  recommendations that would be applicable for better use of A.I

🎯**Key Features**

•	Count of attrition by Job role

•	Average monthly salary

•	Count of attrition by job satisfaction

•	Count of attrition by age group

•	Count of attrition by education field

•	Count of job role population based on job satisfaction.

🛠**Methodology**

The dataset was sourced from Kaggle.
It was then loaded into SQL where all rows missing values  that had missing values were removed together with duplicates.
It was later exported  to Power BI where it was transformed to ensure the datatypes were consistent
To ensure  that the analysis was accurate.
Measures were added and used to create visuals that would speak the results.

🌟**Insights**

•	Use of A.I has diverse impacts on Students depending on how they use it.Those students who use AI constantly but superficially (e.g., relying solely on Direct Answer Generation) often show stagnant or slightly declining grades. Conversely, students who use AI for deep engagement—such as Ideation, Summarizing Reading, or Debugging Code tend to see significant improvements in assignment quality and post-semester GPAs.

•	The higher the  A.I dependency,the lower the retention skill score and thus a high spike in exam  anxiety and increase in burn out risk.Students who completely substitute traditional study hours with AI hours frequently express a high fear of being caught or falling behind conceptually.
