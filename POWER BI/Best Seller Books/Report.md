
**Report of Best-Selling Books in 2024:**

**INTRODUCTION**

A dataset containing 98 Books that were bestsellers in 2023 was analyzed to uncover various factors that contributed in selling of these books. The dataset columns contained the author of the books, prices of the books, book genres, age recommended to read the book, satisfactory rating, number of pages, printing form, book length and years published.

**The dataset was source**
- Kaggle website.
**Tools used in ANALYSIS**
The dataset was entirely analyzed through Microsoft excel. It was cleaned using power query and later loaded into a workbook where various ribbons of excel were later used.
